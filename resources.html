Frequently Asked Questions

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, post a job listing with your project requirements, and we’ll match you with skilled developers who fit your needs. You can review their profiles, check their expertise, and reach out directly to start collaborating.

We offer a wide range of developers, including front-end, back-end, full-stack, mobile app developers, and more. Whether you need expertise in JavaScript, Python, or React, we have developers who specialize in a variety of technologies.

Once you post your job, you’ll be matched with developers who meet your specified criteria. You can review their profiles, skills, work history, and availability to ensure they align with your project needs.

The cost varies depending on the developer’s experience and the type of project. We offer flexible pricing models: hourly rates, fixed-price projects, and long-term contracts. You’ll always have a transparent view of the developer's rates before starting.

Yes! Our developers are available for both short-term and long-term projects. Whether it’s a quick fix or an ongoing partnership, you can hire developers based on the duration and complexity of your project.

Your satisfaction is important to us. If you’re not happy with a developer's performance, we’ll work with you to find a better match or offer an alternative solution to ensure your project runs smoothly.

Our platform offers built-in messaging and communication tools. You can easily discuss project details, set timelines, and provide feedback directly with the developer. We also support scheduling calls if needed.

Once you’ve selected a developer, you’ll finalize the details, including project scope and payment terms. After that, you can start collaborating and managing your project directly through our platform.

Payments are processed through our platform. You can pay based on an hourly rate, project milestones, or a fixed fee. We ensure secure payment methods and clear invoicing for every developer you hire.

You’ll sign a contract with us, which includes all the terms related to hiring a developer. This ensures transparency, security, and a professional relationship for both you and the developer.
